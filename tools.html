⚠️ This Screener Is Missing Critical Analysis

The numbers look good—but you're seeing only 25% of the real deal. Here's what's missing:

1
Financing Costs Not Included
Interest carry, origination points, broker fees, and draw costs typically consume $8–15K per deal. Your profit shrinks 40-60% after real financing is factored in.
2
Closing Costs Are Missing
Title, insurance, taxes, agent commission (typically 6% of ARV), consulting fees—this is $10–20K on acquisition, plus $5–10K at sale.
3
Cash-at-Close Not Calculated
You don't know how much actual capital you need to bring to close. A "$50K profit" deal might require $30–40K out of pocket you don't have.
4
Lender Qualification Not Checked
Your deal might not qualify based on LTV, LTC, or credit score. Different lenders have different criteria—this tool doesn't compare them.
5
No Negotiation Strategy
The full tool shows exactly where to counter, what margin preserves safety, and how each $5K negotiation swing affects your actual return.
6
ROI on Actual Cash Missing
Gross profit isn't return. The full analysis shows your actual ROI on the cash you bring, annualized, and whether it meets your hurdle rate.
